Gorilas

After two hours of walking our guide asked us to leave our bags with the people who helped us carry them as well as the sticks. The trackers were making sounds let them now where they were; the trackers stay with the gorillas from 6 am to 6 pm taking care of them and tracking them. With only the camera, we started to descend a very stiff and slippery cliff in just seconds we had a gorilla in our right side less than 4 meters away a beautiful, peaceful young male eating leaves. Descending more we saw the whole family a mom with her baby and the silver back. It was a wonderful experience.
