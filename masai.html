Masai Village

The Masai people are nomads that live mostly in Kenya and Tanzania; They move around looking for better grass for their cows the most valuable thing they possess, it is how they measure how wealthy each person is. Masais eat cows milk with blood never killing the animal, In special occasions, they will eat a goat's meat. Being nomads, they don't have permanent homes; women build houses made of cow feces inside a manyatta. National parks don't want the Masai's to move around because they destroy and burn a large area of land, so they are helping to make them more permanent by giving them roofs that won't leak and a gas fireplace so they won't burn any more land.
