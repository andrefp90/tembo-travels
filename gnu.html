Gnu Migration

Gnus migrate from Kenya to Tanzania crossing the Masai river looking for grass it is not easy to see they know that every time they cross predators are waiting for them, and many will die. After many days of looking for the great migration, we got very lucky !! One of the gnu's fall the cliff from that moment all the others started to jump off to cross the Masai river; many didn't survive the fall, on the other side of the river lions and leopards, were waiting for them. It was an experience full of mixed emotions it's sad how many die but at the end, you realize it is the circle of life.
